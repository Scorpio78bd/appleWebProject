Everyone Can Create

A new curriculum that
brings creative
expression to every
subject.

When students have more ways to express ideas, it
changes how they think. They see things they wouldn’t
otherwise see and make connections they wouldn’t
otherwise make. With this in mind, we’ve worked with
educators to design a new curriculum that helps you
give students the skills to communicate through
drawing, photography, music, and filmmaking — and
then fold these skills into every subject you teach. So
your students can put math equations to music, bring
an illustrator’s eye to social studies, and make scientific
processes come alive on film.
